Beep Boop!

Hi there, welcome to Beep Boop!
a site that list out a bunch of numbers with a tiny bit of TWIST!

Before we start, here are some rules of the game:

  1. Only numbers allowed!!!
  2. Numbers divisible by 3 are replaced with "I'm sorry, Dave. I'm afraid I can't do that."
  3. Numbers that contain a 1 are replaced with "Boop!".
  4. Numbers that contain a 0 are replaced with "Beep!".

For Example:

If you input 6

"Beep!", "Boop!" , 2 , "I'm sorry, Dave. I'm afraid I can't do that." , 4 , 5 , "I'm sorry, Dave. I'm afraid I can't do that."
